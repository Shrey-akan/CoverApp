About Company

We at ABC handicrafts, a reputable comparty founded in 1990, manufacture, supply, export, and wholesale a wide range of handicraft items and offer
painting services for our clients. Our product line comprises Handcrafted Ganesha statues, Handcrafted Horse Statues, Handcrafted Copper Jugs,
Handcrafted White Metal Handicraft Items, Handcrafted Wooden Ganesha statues, and Meenakari Coconut Ganesha, in addition to Brass Lekar Stone Peacocks and Elephants.

We have achieved remarkable success and are still growing in the industry because of the capable leadership of our mentor and owner, Mr. Hira Lal
Meena. We have carved out a name for ourselves in the market because of his extensive market expertise and grasp of client needs.
